Materials We Work With

Our printer supports a wide range of materials, each optimized for different applications:

  • PLA Eco-friendly, great for display pieces
  • PETG Strong, durable, weather resistant
  • ABS Heat resistant, ideal for functional parts
  • TPU Flexible, rubber-like properties
  • ASA UV resistant, perfect for outdoor use
